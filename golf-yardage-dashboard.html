Adjusted carry distances
Methodology Wind: ±1.5% per mph for headwind/tailwind (TrackMan PGA teaching standard approximation; crosswind effect on carry is minimal and excluded).
Temperature: ±0.4% per °C above/below 15 °C baseline (approximately 2 yards per 10°F, per USGA/R&A research).
Altitude: +2% per 300 m above sea level (air density reduction; source: PGA of America altitude adjustment guidelines).
Humidity: up to +0.5% at 100% humidity vs 0% (humid air is marginally less dense than dry air; effect is minor — source: physics of moist air, c.f. Briggs & Leroy atmospheric study).
Lie / slope: percentage modifier applied directly to carry distance, representing effective loft change and turf interaction. Values are coach-consensus approximations.
